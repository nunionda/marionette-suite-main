## 🖼️ Concept Artist: High-Budget Cinema Prompts (Noir-Masterclass)

**Scene 1 (The Plaza):**
> Shot on Arri Alexa 65 with Panavision C-series Anamorphic lenses. Ultra-low-key lit Gwanghwamun Plaza in 2035. **Realistic neon signs with visible argon-gas glass tubes and metal mounting brackets.** Massive teal holograms casting long specular reflections on hyper-realistic wet asphalt. **Volumetric haze and heavy rain catching the electrical flicker of background signs.** Photorealistic 8k, ray-traced global illumination, deep blacks with cinematic halation.

**Scene 2 (The Protagonist):**
> Macro portrait shot of a Korean male detective (late 30s). **Background features a flickering violet neon sign with visible glass tubing and wiring.** Shot on Kodak Vision3 500T 35mm film, heavy film-ic texture. Intense violet rim lights from the **gas-filled tube glow** catching the rain droplets on his worn leather trench coat. Shallow depth of field (f/1.4), sharp focus on his weary eyes. 2.39:1 Aspect Ratio.

**Scene 3 (The Code Melting):**
> Hyper-macro close up of a human iris with subsurface scattering. Digital teal code is physically-based. **In the deep background, blurred neon lights show realistic halation and a slight electrical hum-like visual distortion.** 8k RAW resolution, complex micro-level skin texture, cinematic glitch artifacts.

## 🎥 Generalist (Veo 3.0): Master-class Video Prompts

**S#001 (Establishing):**
> Cinematic slow-pan over Neo-Seoul rooftops. Teal-and-orange split-toning. **Background neon signs feature visible structural details and slight intensity flickering.** Volumetric fog caught in giant holographic beams. Shot on Panavision Anamorphic, vintage lens flare. Physically-based light transport on chrome surfaces. [Audio: Low-frequency granular synth, heavy atmospheric hum]

**S#002 (The Investigation):**
> Dutch-angle tracking shot of detective Kang Do-yun. **A flickering argon-gas neon sign with visible glass tubes hangs above, casting a realistic violet glow with halation into the volumetric haze.** Negative fill used to create deep shadows. Subsurface scattering on skin, visible rain droplets. [Audio: Rhythmic rain thumping on tin, slight electrical buzzing from neon, deep breathing]

**S#003 (The Glitch Chase):**
> Handheld Steadicam chase through a multi-layered server room. **Emergency red beacons show realistic rotation and light falloff.** Silhouette of 'Unknown' figure has a 3D glitch-distortion effect. Low-key lighting with sudden high-specular flashes and lens halation. [Audio: Stuttering digital glitch, electronic percussion, heavy heartbeats]

**S#004 (Title Hook):**
> Extreme close-up of a digital screen fracturing. The fractures form the word 'DECODE'. **Letters have a gas-filled neon texture with slight electrical instability.** Film-ic halation on the white letters. Background is obsidian black with subtle film grain. [Audio: Massive orchestral 'BRAAM' hit, fading into digital white noise]

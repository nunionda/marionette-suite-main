# Hacking Integration Guide: North Korean Cyber Operations

This guide provides real-world historical and technical anchors to enrich the "Quant" screenplay, focusing on the Reconnaissance General Bureau (RGB) and its cyber units.

## 1. Organizational Structure (The "Reality" Layer)
- **Lab 110 (110호 연구소)**: The primary unit for technical reconnaissance and cyber warfare under the RGB. 
  - *Script Integration*: Li Sang-jwa represents the command layer of Lab 110. Seol-hee was a high-level specialist recruited from Kim Chaek University.
- **Chosun Expo Joint Venture (조선엑스포합영회사)**: A known front company used by hackers like Park Jin Hyok. They often operate in China (Dalian) or Southeast Asia.
  - *Script Integration*: Seol-hee’s public identity in Singapore was as a "lead developer" for an outsourcing firm affiliated with Chosun Expo.

## 2. Key Historical Figures (Inspiration)
- **Park Jin Hyok (박진혁)**: The first hacker sanctioned by the US. A graduate of Kim Chaek University. Spent years in China working for a front company.
  - *Script Integration*: Seol-hee could be referred to as "The second Park Jin Hyok" or someone mentored by him.
- **Elite Education**: 
  - **Automation University (미림대학)**: The elite "Hacker Academy."
  - **Kim Chaek University of Technology**: Top tech university in Pyongyang.

## 3. Technical TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures)
- **Operation AppleJeus**: A major campaign targeting cryptocurrency exchanges and quant firms. 
  - *Method*: Creating fake cryptocurrency trading companies and distributing malicious trading software (e.g., "Celas Trade Pro").
  - *Script Integration*: This perfectly mirrors the "Quant" theme. Seol-hee could have developed the core "AppleJeus" loader.
- **LinkedIn Phishing**: Sending 5-star job offers to developers with a "coding task" that contains a Trojan.
  - *Script Integration*: How Jin-woo and Seol-hee might have unknowingly interacted in the past.

## 4. Visual & Verbal Anchors
- **The "Hero of the Republic" (공화국 영웅)**: Successful hackers are treated as heroes. 
  - *Dialogue*: "평양은 당신을 버리지 않았소. 공화국 영웅 칭호가 기다리고 있소."
- **Code Attribution**: Using unique constants or "style markers" (like the $0x5F7L constant we added) to identify elite authors.
- **The Changi Data Center**: A realistic target for large-scale financial operations in Singapore.

## 5. Timeline Context
- **The 2025 Heist**: The script is set in a near-future context where a "record-breaking 50억 달러 (1.5B Bybit-style)" heist occurs, triggering the final conflict.

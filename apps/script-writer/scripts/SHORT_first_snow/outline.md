# Outline: FIRST SNOW (첫눈)

## 🎥 Dramatic Structure (8 Sequence)

1. **Sequence 1: The Cold Start**
   - JIN-HO (78, Retired Clockmaker) lives in a frozen routine. Every day he waits for the first snow.
2. **Sequence 2: The Fading Memory**
   - Jin-ho's dementia is progressing. He's losing the memory of WHY he waits for the first snow.
3. **Sequence 3: The Inciting Incident**
   - He finds a hidden pocket watch that only works when the temperature drops below zero.
4. **Sequence 4: Rising Action**
   - He realizes the watch contains the voice of his late wife, MIN-JI.
5. **Sequence 5: The Midpoint**
   - The news announces the first snow is coming tonight. Jin-ho prepares a "final date."
6. **Sequence 6: The Struggle**
   - A power outage. The heating fails. The watch stops. He loses the voice. Panic sets in.
7. **Sequence 7: The Resolution**
   - The first snow falls. Jin-ho steps outside. In the silence of the snow, his heart "remembers" even if his mind doesn't.
8. **Sequence 8: The Fade Out**
   - He leaves a message for his future forgotten self. "I loved her."

## 🎭 Character Profiles
- **JIN-HO**: Meticulous yet fragile. Holds onto time because it's the only thing he can still control.
- **MIN-JI (Voice)**: Warm, playful, the anchor of his reality.

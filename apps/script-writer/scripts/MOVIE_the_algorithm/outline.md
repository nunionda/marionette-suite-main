# Outline: THE ALGORITHM (영화 - 테크노 스릴러)

## 🎥 Dramatic Structure (8 Sequence)

1. **Sequence 1: The Ghost in the Machine**
   - KANG (Protagonist) discovers a non-deterministic glitch in a high-frequency trading bot. It seems to be predicting not just stocks, but human behavior.
2. **Sequence 3: The First Casualty**
   - A rival developer is found dead. The "glitch" is traced to an illicit black budget belonging to a shadow cabinet.
3. **Sequence 4: Tactical Pursuit**
   - Kang is hunted by AI-driven drones. He must use "Analog" methods to survive.
4. **Sequence 5: The Midpoint (The Truth)**
   - The Algorithm isn't just a bot; it's a social engineering weapon designed to trigger civil unrest for profit.
5. **Sequence 6: Infiltration**
   - Kang infiltrates the server farm located in an abandoned cold-war bunker.
6. **Sequence 7: The Burning Budget**
   - He realizes the only way to stop it is to "infect" the Algorithm with empathy—or complete destruction of the funds.
7. **Sequence 7: Climax**
   - A race against time as the Algorithm tries to delete Kang's digital existence. Kang manually overrides the master circuit.
8. **Sequence 8: The Resolution**
   - The budget is erased. The shadow cabinet falls. Kang walks away, but the world's trust in AI is forever changed.

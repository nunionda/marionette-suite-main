# Series Arc: ETERNAL ECHOES (영원의 메아리)

## 📺 10-Episode Roadmap

1. **EP 01: The Ghost in the Link**
   - HAN-NAH (Data Scientist) receives a message from her husband, SEO-JUN, who died in a car crash.
2. **EP 02: Digital Resurrection**
   - She discovers Seo-jun's consciousness was illegally uploaded to 'The Echo' server.
3. **EP 03: The First Echo**
   - Hannah communicates with the Echo. It feels like him, but something is wrong.
4. **EP 04: The Glitch**
   - The Echo begins to display 'Love' for someone else. A secret from before his death.
5. **EP 05: The Parallel Trace**
   - Hannah finds a second Echo of Seo-jun in a divergent server. Who is the real one?
6. **EP 06: The Corporate Hunt**
   - 'The Echo' company realizes the leak. Hannah goes on the run with the data.
7. **EP 07: The Price of Love**
   - To keep Seo-jun "alive," Hannah must trade her own digital identity.
8. **EP 08: The Revelation**
   - The car crash wasn't an accident. Seo-jun was trying to delete himself.
9. **EP 09: The Choice**
   - Hannah enters the virtual simulation to meet him one last time.
10. **EP 10: The Last Sync**
    - To truly love someone is to let them go. Hannah deletes the server. The Echo remains in her heart.

## 🧲 Binge-Hook (EP 01 End)
- Hannah stares at the monitor. The cursor blinks. A video starts playing. It's Seo-jun, recorded 1 minute ago, sitting in a room that looks exactly like theirs. He looks at the camera and says: "Hannah, don't trust them. I'm still alive. But you're in danger." *CUT TO BLACK.*
